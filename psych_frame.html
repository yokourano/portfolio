RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am interested in understanding the aesthetic experience through quantitative measures. Specifically, I am interested in what variables in a design contribute to impressions of beauty, cultural variations in aesthetic preferences, and more fundamental topics concerning what structures and circuits in the brain allow humans to experience a visual stimulus as “aesthetically pleasing”.
